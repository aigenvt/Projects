Nelson Mandela

Freedom fighter, statesman, and global icon of peace and reconciliation

Life Timeline

  • 1918

    • Born in Mvezo

    Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela was born in the village of Mvezo in Umtata, South Africa.

  • 1944

    • Joined the ANC

    Became actively involved in the African National Congress and co-founded the ANC Youth League.

  • 1962

    • Arrested and Imprisoned

    Arrested and subsequently sentenced to life imprisonment for sabotage and conspiracy.

  • 1990

    • Released from Prison

    Released after 27 years in prison, signaling the beginning of the end of apartheid.

  • 1993

    • Nobel Peace Prize

    Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize jointly with F.W. de Klerk for their work in peacefully ending apartheid.

  • 1994

    • First Black President

    Elected as South Africa's first Black president in the country's first democratic elections.

  • 2013

    • Passing

    Passed away at age 95, leaving behind an extraordinary legacy of peace and reconciliation.
